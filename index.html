That's me

My name is Gabriel Costa Soares, a passionate and skilled web developer with extensive experience in Vue.js, PHP, Python, and WordPress development. Throughout my career, I’ve honed my ability to create dynamic, user-friendly websites that cater to the specific needs of clients, blending both front-end and back-end technologies seamlessly. I have a solid background in Vue.js and WordPress, which I use for building interactive and real-time web applications, as well as PHP for server-side scripting and automation tasks. My approach combines technical proficiency with a strong focus on user experience, allowing me to deliver high-performance websites that are visually appealing and highly functional.
